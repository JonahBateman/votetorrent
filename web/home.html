Historically, ballots were managed and counted by the community. When voting you might see your neighbors serving as volunteer election judges.

How/when/why did we transition to a system where bureaucrats are in charge of counting the votes?

In 15 seconds:
All it takes is you and your phone.

You are both voter and election judge. So is everyone else.

You can see and verify:

  • the voting totals
  • that you voted and how you voted
  • that your vote is included correctly into the totals
  • who else voted, but not how they voted
  • that everyone is looking at the same votes and totals
